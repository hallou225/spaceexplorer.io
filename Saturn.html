Interesting Facts about Saturn:

  • Saturn is a gas planet, which means you have no surface to stand on.

  • Saturn has the most recorded moons, with a total of 82!

  • A Year on Saturn would be 29 years on Earth.

  • You are actually able to see Saturn through a telescope

  • Saturn has very crazy winds that can reach up to 1,800 kilometres.
