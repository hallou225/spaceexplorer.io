Interesting Facts about Mars:

  • Known as the red planet, from the red rusty colour.

  • Mars has 2 moons called Deimos and Phobos.

  • Due to the hard and rocky surface, Mars is a Terrestrial planet.

  • Mars has violent dust storms caused by the sun, which can last months.
